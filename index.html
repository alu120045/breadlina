Protoype

This bread contains inulin-propionate, a substance that, when consumed, makes you feel full even after consuming only a modest amount. People who struggle with hunger will benefit from it. To avoid any additional unfavorable consequences, the chemical will be incorporated into the substances during the procedure. A substance called inulin-propionate that suppresses libitum energy intake while also increasing the release of satiety hormones and preventing weight gain. To get IPE to function, it has to be incorporated into a widely tolerated food system, bread in this instance.

The main ingredient that will be used to achieve the desired product is inulin propionate fiber, which is extracted from the root of the archery. Inulin propionate is a chemical that suppresses libitum energy intake, but also increases the release of satiety hormones and prevents weight gain. For EIP to work, it must be incorporated into a widely tolerated food system, which would be bread in this case.
